## Threatened species

* Estimated % of threatened species in 2021 according to IUCN Red List version 2021-3
  * mammals, birds, reptiles, amphibians, horseshoe crabs and gymnosperms 
* Data source: [iucnredlist.org](https://www.iucnredlist.org/)
